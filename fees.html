Investment & Financial Support

NATI-RETI 2026 is committed to fostering a diverse community of emerging leaders from across the United States, Canada, and beyond. To maintain the high academic and residential quality of the Institute, the following fee structure and financial policies apply.

Fee Structure

CA$ 200
US$ 150
Registration Fee
  • Due upon acceptance to the program.
  • This fee is non-refundable and is required to officially confirm your participation and secure your place in the Institute.
CA$ 2,500
US$ 1,800
Program Fee
  • Covers the full cost of the residential phase, including study materials, shared accommodation, and all meals.
  • Included Transport & Visits: This fee specifically covers transportation from Detroit to Toronto during the program and all scheduled study visits and local program excursions.
✈

Travel & Incidental Costs

Participants are responsible for arranging and funding their own:

  • Arrival & Departure Travel: Transportation from your home location to Detroit (program start) and from Toronto back to your home location (program conclusion).
  • Documentation: All costs associated with passports and visas required for entry into both the United States and Canada.
  • Insurance: Mandatory travel and health insurance for the duration of the program.
✦

Financial Assistance

We believe that financial constraints should not prevent qualified leaders from participating. Thanks to the generous support of our sponsoring denominations, need-based financial assistance is available to help cover the Program Fee (CA$ 2,500 / US$ 1,800).

  • Eligibility: Assistance is granted based on demonstrated financial need and denominational representation.
  • Personal Commitment: Please note that financial assistance generally does not cover the Registration Fee (CA$ 200 / US$ 150) or personal travel expenses to Detroit and from Toronto. Applicants are encouraged to seek additional sponsorship from their home churches or academic institutions to cover these costs.
  • How to Apply: You can indicate your request for financial support directly on the Application Form.

Ready to take the next step? Submit your application and indicate your need for financial support on the form.
